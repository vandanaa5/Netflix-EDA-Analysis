# Netflix EDA Project

# Project Overview
This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a cleaned Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset. The goal is to uncover patterns, trends, and insights that help understand Netflix’s content distribution and growth.

---

# Dataset Information
- **Dataset Name:** Netflix Movies and TV Shows  
- **Source:** Kaggle  
- **Total Records:** ~8800  
- **Type:** Movies & TV Shows metadata  

---

# Objectives
- Analyze content distribution (Movies vs TV Shows)  
- Identify top contributing countries  
- Study content growth over time  
- Examine ratings and genres  
- Derive meaningful business insights  

---

# Tools & Technologies
- Python  
- Pandas  
- Matplotlib  

---

# Key Analysis Performed

### 🔹 Content Distribution
- Compared number of Movies and TV Shows  

### 🔹 Country-wise Analysis
- Identified top countries producing content  

### 🔹 Ratings Analysis
- Studied most common audience ratings  

### 🔹 Time-based Analysis
- Analyzed content growth over years  
- Observed trends in content addition  

### 🔹 Genre Analysis
- Identified most popular genres  

### 🔹 Duration Analysis
- Analyzed distribution of movie durations  

---

# Key Insights
- Movies dominate over TV Shows on Netflix  
- Content increased significantly after 2016  
- United States contributes the highest number of titles  
- TV-MA and TV-14 are the most common ratings  
- Drama and International genres are most popular  

---

# Conclusion
The EDA highlights Netflix’s rapid growth, global reach, and diverse content strategy. The insights help understand how content is distributed across regions, genres, and audience categories.
